Sanae Kochiya, the Wind Priestess of Moriya Shrine in Touhou Project, now comes to the DS world!
Sanae is a human, the Wind Priestess of Moriya Shrine, but she is also a distant descendant of the Suwako Moriya, one of the goddesses of Moriya Shrine. She is in charge of gathering faith for the gods, and she also became a minor deity with the power of faith. She has the power of summoning gods of Moriya Shrine and causing miracles to occur. Since the faith competition in Gensokyo is rather drastic, she decides to come to the DS world to tame the residents and gather their faith. Can she survive and successfully gather more faith?
Note: This mod is  the beta version, so it might contain bugs. If you find one, please report it as detailed as you can in the comments below, so that I can try to fix the bug!
-Stats-
Health: 120
Hunger: 90
Sanity: 200
Max Faith: 100
-Perks-
[Living God]
Sanae is both a human and a god. She can gather faith from others and enhance her power. She has a unique stat of Faith. When there are pigmen, bunnymen and players around, she can collect faith slowly from them. Sanae can also build shrines to collect faith remotely. The higher her current faith is, the higher her walking/attacking speed, damage and resistance, and the slower she starves. The spellcards and skills of Sanae also cost faith. In order to maintain her godship,  Sanae's faith will degrade naturally at the rate of 0.2/s.
[Youkai Exterminator]
One of the duties of Wind Priestess is youkai extermination. Sanae has witnessed a variety of horrors and is fearless against the monsters in DS world. She will not lose sanity facing monsters, but as a normal girl, she's still afaid of the darkness.
-Spellcards-
Miracle「Night with Overly Bright Guest Stars」
Sanae summons a guest star above her head. The guest star will follow Sanae, providing a large range of light and warming the players  nearby. The guest star will last for 2 minutes. If it's raining when casting the spellcard, it will also clear the weather. This spellcard costs 20 faith and can be used twice.
Miracle「Miracle Fruit」
Sanae summons 6 miracle fruits around her. It is a veggie type of food, and can restore 5 health, 10 sanity and 8 hunger when eaten. Players who eat the miracle fruit will restore extra 3 health and 5 sanity when eating other veggie of seed fruits within 5 minutes. This spellcard costs 30 faith and can be used once.
Seance「Froggy Braves the Wind and Rain」
Sanae tries to summon the follower frogs of Suwako, but it appears that something goes wrong...Activates a frog rain that lasts for 30 seconds. This spellcard costs 20 faith and can be used once.
-Skills-
Sky-God Summon
Press Z key to activate. Sanae summons the power of Kanako Yasaka, the wind god of Moriya Shrine, creating an onbashira that will fall to the position of mouse 2 secods later. When the onbashira lands, all non-player entities(PVE mode)/all entities except Sanae(PVP mode) will take 400 physical damage. The onbashira will then disappear slowly. This skill costs 40 faith and has a cooldown of 30 seconds.
Earth-God Summon
Press X key to activate. Sanae summons the power of Suwako Moriya, the mountains god of  Moriya Shrine, creating a bubble shield around Sanae. The bubble shield will absorb 400 damage for Sanae, and can persist up to 30 seconds. However, Sanae will still get stunned when attacked. This skill costs 30 faith and has a cooldown of 30 seconds.
-Items-
Wind Priestess' Gohei
: The equipment for shrine rituals in Moriya Shrine, blessed by Kanako and Suwako to become exceptionally light and strong. It deals a melee damage of 20, and increases the walkspeed of the holder by 30%. When Sanae is holding the gohei, she can rightclick the gohei to activate Miracle「God's Wind」. When the skill is activated, Sanae's attack will become ranged, and each attack will summon a tornado at the target, but each attack also costs 10 faith. When Sanae's faith falls beneath 10, the skill will automatically switch off.
Counterfait Suwako's Hat
: A mimic product of Suwako Moriya's Hat that has a bit of Suwako's power on it. It can provide some insulation and waterproofness, and 25% damage reduction. Players which equip  the hat can rightclick the hat to hide underground, to avoid being descovered by enemies.
Crystal of Faith
:A crystal of courage and hope, purified from the nightmare. Sanae can consume the crystal and restore 30 faith. So it is that shrine maiden seeketh faiths.
-Buildings-
Small Branch of Moriya Shrine
A small shrine that is just for visiting activities. This shrine can collect faith from pigmen/bunnymen/players within 20 yards at the rate of 0.2/s, and send the faith to Sanae, wherever she is.
Branch of Moriya Shrine
A proper large shrine for praying and offering activities. This shrine can collect faith from pigmen/bunnymen/players within 30 yards at the rate of 0.4/s, and send the faith to Sanae, wherever she is. Players can offer fuels to the shrine to let it provide lighting. Players can also offer gems, gold, meat and prepared food at the shrine to obtain grace of the gods...or sometime penalties, depending on what you offered. This function has a cooldown of 30 seconds.
Note: Multiple shrines cannot collect faith more than once from a single prayer, and faith will be offered to the larger shrine in priority. If there are multiple players of Sanae in the map, the faith collected by shrines will be equally devided.
Configuration:
-Language: switch between English and Chinese
-Max Health/Sanity/Hunger: To set the maximum of Sanae's stats
-Horizontal/Vertical Position of Faith Bar: To change the position of Sanae's faith bar in your interface
